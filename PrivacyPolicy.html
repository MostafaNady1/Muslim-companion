Privacy Policy for Muslim Companion

Muslim Companion ("we", "our", or "the app") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how the app handles information when you use it.

1. Information We Access

Location Information

The app requests approximate location (Coarse Location) permission while the app is in use for the following features:

  • Calculating prayer times
  • Determining Qiblah direction

Your location is processed only on your device and may be stored locally on your phone for future use. No location data is sent to our servers.

Audio Files

The app may download Quran audio files (Ayat recitations) to your device storage so they can be played later offline.

Saved Images

The app may save images to your device gallery using Android system features. No storage permission is explicitly requested by the app.

2. Advertising

The app displays advertisements including:

  • Banner Ads
  • Interstitial Ads
  • Rewarded Ads

Ads may be provided by third-party advertising services such as Google AdMob. These services may collect certain device information or usage data in accordance with their own privacy policies.

3. Data Sharing

We do not collect, upload, sell, or share your personal data with our own servers.

4. Data Storage

Any saved settings, downloaded audio, and locally stored location preferences remain on your device only.

5. Children's Privacy

The app is not intended to knowingly collect personal information from children.

6. Third-Party Services

The app may use third-party services such as Google AdMob which may process data under their own policies.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page.
